# NCLEX Prep Nation — Trust, Legal, Disclaimer, and Compliance Guide

## 1. Document Purpose

This document defines the trust, legal, disclaimer, content integrity, privacy, refund, and ethical positioning requirements for NCLEX Prep Nation.

The purpose is to make the website look legitimate, honest, and safe for students.

This document is not a substitute for professional legal advice. Before launch, the business owner should review the legal pages with a qualified legal professional, especially if the platform will sell digital materials internationally.

---

## 2. Trust Is a Core Product Feature

For NCLEX Prep Nation, trust is not optional.

Students will only request packages or share contact details if they believe:

- The website is real.
- The content is original.
- The platform is not pretending to be official.
- The platform does not sell leaked questions.
- Their contact information is safe.
- The package process is clear.
- There is a real support channel.
- The refund/manual payment policy is understandable.

Trust must be visible on every major page.

---

## 3. Main Trust Positioning

NCLEX Prep Nation should position itself as:

> An independent NCLEX preparation platform that provides original practice questions, study resources, rationales, mock exams, NGN-style case studies, and guided support for nursing candidates.

The website must not position itself as:

- The official NCLEX website
- An official NCSBN website
- A Pearson VUE website
- A nursing regulatory body website
- A seller of real exam questions
- A seller of leaked exam materials
- A guaranteed-pass shortcut

---

## 8. No Guarantee Statement

The website should not promise guaranteed results.

Recommended statement:

> NCLEX Prep Nation provides study tools, practice questions, resources, and support to help candidates prepare. However, exam results depend on each candidate’s preparation, knowledge, performance, eligibility, and exam conditions. We do not guarantee that use of the platform will result in passing the NCLEX.

Avoid:

- Guaranteed pass
- 100% pass rate
- Pass or your money back, unless legally reviewed
- Real exam questions
- Secret NCLEX questions

NCLEX Prep Nation is an independent educational preparation platform created to support nursing candidates preparing for NCLEX-RN and NCLEX-PN exams.

NCLEX® is a registered trademark of the National Council of State Boards of Nursing, Inc. NCLEX Prep Nation is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by NCSBN, Pearson VUE, or any nursing regulatory body.

All practice questions, NGN-style case studies, rationales, mock exams, study guides, and resources provided by NCLEX Prep Nation are original educational materials created for exam preparation purposes only.

## 15. Refund Policy Requirements

URL: `/refund-policy`

Because the platform uses manual payments, refund terms must be clear.

### 15.1 Refund Policy Should Explain

- How users request a refund
- What purchases are refundable
- What purchases may be non-refundable
- Timeframe for refund requests
- How digital materials affect refunds
- How guided support refunds work
- Contact method for refund requests

### 15.2 Digital Materials Note

Digital products are often treated differently because access can be delivered immediately.

Recommended wording:

> Because digital resources and question bank access may be delivered immediately after payment confirmation, some payments may be non-refundable after access has been granted or materials have been sent. Refund requests will be reviewed according to the package type, delivery status, and support already provided.

### 15.3 Manual Refund Process

Refund process:

1. Student contacts support.
2. Admin reviews request.
3. Admin checks access/material delivery status.
4. Admin approves or denies based on policy.
5. If approved, admin records refund.
6. Admin updates package request status to Refunded.
7. Student is notified.

## 18. Manual Payment Trust Language

Because payment is manual, the wording must be careful.

### 18.1 Good Manual Payment Wording

Use:

> Submit your package request and our team will contact you by WhatsApp or email with package details, payment instructions, and access confirmation.

Use:

> Access is activated after payment confirmation.

Use:

> You may contact us directly on WhatsApp if you want help choosing the right package.

### 18.2 Bad Manual Payment Wording

Avoid:

- Payment system not ready
- We do not have online payment
- Payment unavailable
- Pay anyhow
- Send money first
- No refund
- Trust us
